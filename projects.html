Projects

We produce a report based on our analysis of the business. Below is the basic deliverable structure.

Report section

Executive summary

What we studied, what we found, and what we recommend—at a glance.

Report section

Findings

Observed issues and opportunities, supported by evidence from discovery.

Report section

Recommendations

Actionable steps with rationale and suggested sequencing (what to do first).

Typical deliverables

  • Process overview (how work flows today)
  • Opportunity/bottleneck analysis
  • Options comparison (if multiple paths exist)
  • Recommended plan + next steps
  • Appendix: interview notes, supporting data, references
