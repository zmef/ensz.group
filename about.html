About ENSZ

We’re a Eureka College Business Capstone consulting team focused on clear analysis and useful recommendations.

Our approach

  • Respect the real world. Time and attention are limited, so we keep our process simple.
  • Write for action. Our report is designed to be used, not just read.
  • Make reasoning visible. We show what we observed, what it implies, and why we recommend what we recommend.
